How Lefto Works

Saving money and the planet is as easy as 1-2-3.

1. Browse Deals

Hotels post their high-quality surplus food at the end of the day or after events at a steep discount.

2. Claim & Pay

Select the quantity you want, claim the deal, and pay securely online via Razorpay to reserve your food.

3. Pick Up & Enjoy

Head to the hotel at the designated time, show your order confirmation, and enjoy a premium meal!
